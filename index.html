5 HR Compliance Pitfalls Every Startup Should Avoid

Startups often move fast, but ignoring HR compliance can lead to costly legal issues and decreased employee morale. Here are five pitfalls to avoid:

  1. Improper Employee Classification: Misclassifying employees as independent contractors or exempt vs. non-exempt can result in fines and back pay.
  2. Incomplete Documentation: Failure to maintain proper I-9 forms, handbooks, and job descriptions exposes businesses to legal vulnerabilities.
  3. Ignoring Wage and Hour Laws: Ensure proper overtime calculations, rest breaks, and record-keeping to stay compliant with FLSA and local labor laws.
  4. Lack of Harassment Prevention Training: Preventing a toxic culture and avoiding liability starts with required anti-harassment policies and training.
  5. No Written Policies or Inconsistent Enforcement: Clear, consistently applied policies prevent discrimination claims and build employee trust.
